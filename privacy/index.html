Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to SpendFlow ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application, SpendFlow.

Last Updated: April 16, 2026

Key Takeaway: SpendFlow is designed with privacy-first principles. All your financial data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal financial information on any external servers.

Information We Collect

SpendFlow is a local-first application. Here's what you should know about data collection:

Data Type Collection Storage Location
Transaction Records By user input Local device only
Account Information By user input Local device only
Budget Settings By user input Local device only
Categories By user input Local device only
Usage Analytics None N/A
Personal Identifiers None N/A

How We Use Your Information

The information you enter into SpendFlow is used solely for:

  • Tracking your income and expenses
  • Calculating and displaying your budget status
  • Organizing transactions by categories
  • Providing insights into your spending patterns
  • Enabling data export functionality

We do not use your data for advertising, marketing, or any third-party purposes.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored locally on your iOS device using Apple's Core Data framework, which provides:

  • Secure local storage with iOS built-in encryption
  • Protection through iOS sandbox architecture
  • Optional Face ID / Touch ID authentication

We do not have access to your data, and your financial information never leaves your device.

Biometric Authentication

SpendFlow supports Face ID and Touch ID for app security. When you enable this feature:

  • Biometric authentication is handled entirely by iOS
  • We do not collect or store your biometric data
  • Authentication occurs locally on your device

Data Export

When you use the CSV export feature:

  • Your data is processed locally on your device
  • Exported files are saved to your chosen location
  • You have full control over how exported files are shared or stored

Third-Party Services

SpendFlow does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. Your data stays on your device.

Children's Privacy

SpendFlow is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since all data is stored locally and we do not collect any information, our app is safe for general use.

Data Retention

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Data is retained as long as you keep the app installed
  • Deleting the app will remove all stored data
  • You can manually delete individual records at any time

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is available within the app
  • Correction: Edit any record at any time
  • Deletion: Delete individual records or all data
  • Export: Export your data to CSV format
  • Portability: Take your exported data anywhere

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
