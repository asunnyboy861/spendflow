About SpendFlow

SpendFlow is a real-time budget tracker designed to help you see how much you can spend at a glance. Our app focuses on simplicity and efficiency, making budget tracking effortless.

  • Track income and expenses with ease
  • Manage multiple accounts (bank, credit, cash)
  • Set daily, weekly, or monthly budgets
  • Organize transactions by categories
  • View real-time budget remaining
  • Secure your data with Face ID / Touch ID
  • Export your data to CSV format

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

All your financial data is stored locally on your device using Apple's Core Data framework. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal financial information on external servers.

Is my data secure?

Yes! Your data never leaves your device. SpendFlow uses Apple's built-in security features and supports Face ID and Touch ID for additional protection.

How do I set up a budget?

Go to Settings → Budget Settings to configure your daily, weekly, or monthly budget. You can also set category-specific budgets for more detailed tracking.

Can I export my transactions?

Yes! Navigate to Settings → Export CSV to download your transaction history in a spreadsheet-compatible format.

How do I add a new account?

Tap the Accounts tab and use the "+" button to add bank accounts, credit cards, or cash wallets. Each account can be customized with a name, type, and starting balance.
